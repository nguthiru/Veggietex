ABOUT US

VEGGIETEX LIMITED offers a wide range of fresh vegetables and fruits in both local and international markets covering the basic day to day necessities as well as new innovative food solutions that cater to consumers needs.

Our products are fresh from our farms with certified global quality standards. With high-quality, we are a reliable processor and exporter who supply to Scandinavia, UK, Europe & UAE.

OUR MISSION

1. To delight our consumers worldwide of products with superior quality and value
2. To actively participate in the industrialization of the agricultural sector
3. To continually improve our business processes
4. To contribute to the growth of society as we grow the companys business.

OUR VISION

To become one of the top globally impacting agricultural produce companies enriching consumers lives with healthy, high quality and innovative products.

OUR VALUES

1. SERVICE

Our focus is on providing solutions to customers needs. With a view to improving customer satisfaction, we have always placed high importance on product knowledge.


2. INTEGRITY

Respect for our customers, employees, the public, and the environment are core parts of our values and beliefs.


3. QUALTIY

Our key goal is to provide the best products possible to our customers and consumers.


4. GUIDELINE

Our business strategy is WIN- WIN for us and with whom we do business transaction. Knowledge is our base, integrity is our path, and satisfaction is our focus.

CORPORTE PHILOSOPHY

We will achieve our vision by upholding firmly to the following corporate philosophy:

1. Our People: - We are very passionate about our people and our company culture. We believe that by searching for, acquiring, and developing the best talent, we will always have the team that strives to deliver the highest standards.

2. Our Partners: - Our suppliers are our partners. We insist on establishing long-term partnerships with our suppliers and integrate the farms into our production cycle.

3. Our Products: - by hand-picking the raw material, utilizing expert technical knowledge & advanced machinery, and controlling the cycle end to end, we ensure that our products meet the highest quality and food safety standards.

4. Our Service: - with a very diversified global portfolio, we offer a personalized customer experience to maximize satisfaction and to efficiently anticipate and react to our customers needs.

5.To the Future: - A commitment to the vision necessary to meet the challenges of a rapidly changing world and creativity to take advantage of opportunities through continuity of effort and singleness of purpose, maintaining our leadership in agricultural products exportation.

SAFETY, HEALTH AND ENVIRONMENT

- Health, Safety and Environment aware-ness is major priority for us. The Company has thus developed the HSE Policy, HSE Action Plan and Employer/Employee Safety Charter as instruments of managing Health, Safety and Environmental Protection throughout the whole Organization.
